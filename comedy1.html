Saitama, though initially unimpressive in appearance, harbors a distinctive passion: being a hero. Driven by his childhood dream, he dedicatedly underwent three years of rigorous training, resulting in the loss of all his hair. Now possessing unparalleled strength, he can effortlessly defeat any adversary with a single punch. However, the absence of worthy opponents has left Saitama in an unexpected predicament—he finds himself unable to relish the thrill of combat and is plagued by boredom.

One fateful day, 19-year-old cyborg Genos notices Saitama's extraordinary abilities and aspires to become his disciple. Genos suggests they join the Hero Association to gain official recognition for their positive impact on society. Surprised that his identity is unknown to the public, Saitama readily agrees. As they encounter new comrades and confront fresh adversaries, Saitama embarks on a new chapter as a Hero Association member, seeking to rediscover the excitement of battle that once fueled his passion
