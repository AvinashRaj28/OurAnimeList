Edo, once a city pulsating with the vitality and dreams of samurai nationwide, now finds those aspirations dashed after the surrender of feudal Japan to formidable extraterrestrials called the "Amanto." With the influential shogunate reduced to a puppet government, a new decree swiftly outlaws the public possession of all swords.

In steps Gintoki Sakata, an unconventional silver-haired individual who defiantly wields a wooden sword, maintaining his samurai identity despite the prohibition. Serving as the founder of Yorozuya, a small enterprise specializing in odd jobs, Gintoki frequently undertakes ventures to aid others, albeit in peculiar and unexpected ways.
Teamed up with Shinpachi Shimura, a bespectacled youth supposedly learning the samurai path; Kagura, a spirited girl with superhuman strength and an insatiable appetite; and Sadaharu, their colossal pet dog with a penchant for biting heads, the Yorozuya faces everything from encounters with alien royalty to skirmishes with local gangs in the ever-evolving landscape of Edo.
